Extra Speed Oil

"HSK Lube Pvt. Ltd." started its operation a few years ago. We are currently engaged in the manufacturing and trading of high-quality Engine Oils, Gear Oils, Hydraulic Oils. Front Fork Oils , Greases and other lubricant products .

Our products have an excellent market standing due to their superior quality and long self life. Products are manufactured under high quality production guidelines and pass stringent quality checks before being marked fit for consumption .

We offer our products in superior quality and attractive packaging . Special care is taken in preparing our products for the transport process so as to minimize damage during shipping . We are currently marketing our latest range of high quality lubricant products under the brand name of "XPEEDOL" in collaboration with "Progressive Enterprises" as our international marketing partner .

We Have tied up with our skilled logisticpartners so as to ensure that our productss reaches our customers on time and in excellent condition . We welcome you to join us in this exciting journey .
